your focus for the day

The Catalyst Challenges

You will work on one of two real challenges from the Catalyst programme. We will split into groups on the day, so think about where your experience can add the most value and which challenge you would most like to pick up.

Challenge 1

Economic Crime Prevention Platform

Role-based AI enablement

Develop high-value, role-specific copilot prompts for ECPP role groups, including engineers, Consumer Journey Managers and product owners. The goal is useful AI support designed around the work each role actually does.

Potential impact
  • Faster decision-making
  • Improved productivity
  • More consistent use of AI across ECPP
Challenge 2

Operating Model Delivery

Better performance goals

Use AI to review more than 600 performance goals, checking that they follow Your Best guidance and link clearly to the 2026 priorities.

The opportunity
  • SharePoint-based AI agents providing consistent quality control
  • Stronger goals and action plans tailored to delivery roles
  • Consistent checks across teams at scale

Before the hackathon...

If you can, speak to people in your teams and gather useful detail about these challenges. Think about the people involved, what currently works, where the friction is and what could be improved. Better context will make the planning more effective and the solution stronger. You do not need to arrive with the answer — come ready to ask good questions and contribute your experience.

Which challenge? Where can you add value? What can your team tell you?
