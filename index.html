Set up Base44 before you arrive

We'll build with Base44 in your groups — a free AI app-builder where you describe what you want and it builds it. Create your account at base44.com before the day, so we spend the time building, not signing up.

⚠️ A note on credits. Base44's free plan gives you a small credit allowance, and it gets used up quickly — so feel free to explore before the day, but don't rely on your own credits on the day. The business-problem build is facilitator-led on a paid plan, so there'll be plenty of credits for everyone.
